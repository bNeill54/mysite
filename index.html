My coaching approach

We keep it simple: diagnose the cause, pick 1–2 priorities, then build a plan you’ll actually do between sessions.

Step 1

Assess

Your typical miss, strike pattern, and start line — not just “how it looks.”

Step 2

Simplify

One feel + one drill. No clutter, no overload.

Step 3

Transfer

Practice structure + on-course cues so your range swing becomes your playing swing.

Who it’s for
