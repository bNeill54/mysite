My coaching approach

We keep it simple: diagnose the cause, pick 1–2 priorities, then build a practice plan you’ll actually do.

Step 1

Assess

Your typical miss, strike pattern, and start lines — not just “how it looks.”

Step 2

Simplify

One feel + one drill. You shouldn’t need 10 swing thoughts.

Step 3

Transfer

Practice structure + on-course cues so your range swing becomes your playing swing.

Who it’s for

A good fit if you want practical coaching and steady progress.

  • Beginners who want a solid foundation
  • Golfers stuck with a slice / hook / inconsistent contact
  • Players who “look good on the range” but can’t score
  • Anyone who wants a simple practice plan and accountability
